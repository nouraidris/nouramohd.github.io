2020 to a Negro

2020 to a Negro is a video and audio collage that consolidates all the tragic incidents of 2020 in the life of Black people. This video is for Americans to realize that until racism is truly dealt with, America as a whole will be affected.

As a black women myself, I have faced my own share of racism and hatred over the years. This year however it was revealed to us all that the hate and prejuidce still persists in some Americans which only resulted to mayhem. The beginning of the video highlights the effects of inclusivness and unity. It displays liberty which was promised to all of America, the following clips after however, reveal the truth we live in today. It displays anger, chaos and inequality. Till this day, black people are begging for the right to live.
